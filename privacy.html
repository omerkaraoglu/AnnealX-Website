Privacy Policy

readark — 3D Print Annealer. Last updated: May 2026.

· Overview

readark OÜ (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a company registered in Estonia that designs and sells the readark 3D Print Annealer, a standalone hardware device for thermally annealing 3D-printed parts. The device is manufactured in Türkiye by Elektromag Makine on behalf of readark. We are committed to transparency about how our products interact with your data.

In short: we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from you or your device.

· What data the device processes

The readark 3D Print Annealer is a standalone appliance. All operational data — temperature readings, thermal profiles, calibration settings, and custom profiles — is stored locally on the device's non-volatile memory (NVS) and never leaves the device.

WiFi credentials

If you choose to connect the device to your WiFi network, the network name (SSID) and password you enter are stored exclusively on the device's local memory. These credentials are never transmitted to readark or any third party. They are used solely to establish a local network connection for the purpose described below.

Firmware updates (OTA)

When connected to WiFi, the device periodically checks for firmware updates by contacting a version manifest file hosted on our website (readark.eu). During this check:

  • The device sends a standard HTTPS request to download a small text file (the version manifest).
  • No personal data, device identifiers, serial numbers, usage data, telemetry, or analytics are transmitted.
  • Our web server may log standard access data (such as IP addresses) as part of normal server operation. These logs are used solely for server maintenance and security purposes, are not linked to any individual or device, and are automatically deleted within 30 days.

No other network communication occurs. The device does not phone home, transmit telemetry, report usage statistics, or communicate with any server for any other purpose.

· What we do not collect

To be explicit, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or serial numbers
  • Usage data, temperature logs, or thermal profiles
  • Location data
  • Analytics or behavioural data
  • Any data whatsoever from your device

· Cookies and website

Our website (readark.eu) does not use tracking cookies, analytics services, or advertising pixels. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

· Third-party services

The firmware update manifest and firmware binary files are hosted on our own website (readark.eu). We do not rely on third-party services for the operation of the device. Our website hosting provider may process standard server log data (such as IP addresses) as part of normal infrastructure operation; this is governed by the hosting provider's own privacy practices and is not under our direct control.

· Children's privacy

Our product is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

· Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK / Law No. 6698), you have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection.

Because we do not collect or process any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you have questions or concerns about this, you are welcome to contact us.

· Changes to this policy

If we ever introduce features that involve the collection or processing of personal data, we will update this policy before those features are deployed and will clearly communicate the changes. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions.
