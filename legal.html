02 Product use and safety

2.1 Intended use

The readark 3D Print Annealer is designed exclusively for the thermal annealing of 3D-printed polymer parts within the temperature range and material types specified in the product documentation. It is intended for use by adults in a domestic or workshop environment.

2.2 Important safety information

The device contains a heating element that operates at temperatures up to 200 °C. Users must:

  • Read and follow all safety instructions provided with the product before first use.
  • Never leave the device unattended while a thermal profile is running.
  • Place the device on a stable, heat-resistant surface away from flammable materials.
  • Keep the device out of reach of children.
  • Not modify the device hardware, wiring, or mains connection in any way.
  • Use the device only with the power supply and voltage specified for your region.

2.3 Misuse

readark accepts no responsibility for damage, injury, or loss arising from use of the product in a manner inconsistent with the product documentation, including but not limited to:

  • Operation outside the specified temperature range or with unsupported materials.
  • Unauthorised hardware modification, tampering, or repair by unqualified persons.
  • Use in environments or conditions not described in the product documentation.
  • Continued use of a device that is visibly damaged or malfunctioning.
